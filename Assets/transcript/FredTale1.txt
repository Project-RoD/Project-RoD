The Witcher Chapter 1. Later it was said the man came from the north, from Roper's gate. He came off with leading his laden horse by the bridle. It was late afternoon and the Roper's, hadlers and tenors. The stalls were already closed. The street empty. It was hot but the man had the black coat thrown over his shoulder. He drew attention to himself. He stopped in front of the old narrow cart in, stood there for a moment listening to the hubbub of voices as usual. At this hour it was full of people. The stranger did not enter the old narrow cart. He pulled his horse further down the street to another cabin, a smaller one called the fox. Not enjoying the best repetition. It was almost empty.
